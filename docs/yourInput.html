How to add your own Bitmoji

  • Open the Bitmoji App on Chrome or on your mobile device.

  • Select any Bitmoji image, right click it, and click "copy image address".

  • Or you can drag the image into a browser window and copy the URL from the browser.

  • Paste the Bitmoji URL into our Bitmoji Avatar extractor tool. You're done!
